### ✅ Task 1 – ESG Regulatory Strategy & Policy Analysis

**Objective:**
Analyze upcoming EU sustainability regulations and identify key ESG requirements and 2026 priorities to support Alive’s sustainability strategy.

**Work Completed:**

* Reviewed ESPR, CSDDD, and the Green Claims Directive, extracting five critical regulatory requirements
* Mapped regulatory requirements to GRI 308 and GRI 414 supplier assessment standards
* Identified three strategic priorities for 2026: CSRD automation, EU Taxonomy readiness, and AI emissions governance
* Summarized findings in structured Excel sheets with priorities, actions, and references

### ✅ Task 3 – Grant Budget Structuring & Immediate Action Planning

**Objective:**
Design a focused €100,000 financial plan (with €50,000 grant-funded) aligned to the FoundingBONUS Plus program and extract the most critical items from the €3M budget into a lean, high-impact allocation.

**Work Completed:**

* Reviewed the FoundingBONUS Plus requirements and aligned them with Alive’s immediate financial needs
* Shortlisted essential items from the €3M budget: R&D, early sales, compliance, talent, and IP/legal
* Allocated the €100k budget across five key categories: Product & Tech, Marketing & Early Sales, Sustainability & Compliance, Personnel, and IP & Legal
* Divided all costs into two groups: grant-funded (50%) and company-funded (50%)
* Added immediate action items, 0–3 month timelines, and detailed execution notes for grant implementation
